Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Data Minimization Commitment

Senatai is committed to protecting your privacy through data minimization. We only collect:

  • Your username (no email required)
  • Your encrypted password
  • Your voting patterns and Policap balance

How Your Data Is Used

Your anonymized voting data contributes to:

  • Aggregate consensus views on legislation
  • Research on democratic participation
  • Revenue for the cooperative data trust

Your Rights

  • You can delete your account at any time
  • Your individual votes are never sold or shared
  • Only anonymized, aggregated data is used commercially
  • You receive dividends from data trust revenue

Cooperative Agreement & Data Trust

Cooperative Ownership Model

By joining Senatai, you become a member-owner of the Senatai Cooperative. This means:

  • You own your data and control how it's used
  • You participate in cooperative governance decisions
  • You receive dividend payments based on your engagement

Political Capital (Policap) System

Policap is our cooperative currency that represents your civic engagement:

  • Earn Policap by voting on legislation (1.0 Policap per survey)
  • Daily limits apply: Full rewards for first 10 votes, then diminishing returns
  • Spend up to 2 Policap per bill to emphasize your position
  • Your Policap balance determines your dividend share

Data Trust Revenue Model

The Senatai Data Trust generates revenue by selling anonymized, aggregated civic engagement data to:

  • Academic researchers studying democratic participation
  • Policy institutes analyzing public opinion
  • Media organizations understanding constituent views

All revenue is distributed to cooperative members based on their Policap balance.

Disclaimers

  • Senatai is NOT affiliated with any government entity
  • Your votes here do NOT count as official votes in elections
  • Vote predictions are estimates and may not be accurate
  • Dividend amounts are estimates and not guaranteed
  • This platform is for civic education and engagement only
