Vote Predictor Modules

AI-powered modules that predict your vote based on your history and preferences

How Vote Predictors Work

Vote predictor modules analyze your voting patterns and use machine learning to predict how you might vote on new legislation:

  • Training Data: Your historical votes and preferences
  • Predictions: Includes confidence score for each prediction
  • Transparency: View the evidence used for each prediction
  • Control: You can always override predictions

⚠️ Important Disclaimers

  • Predictions are estimates and may not be accurate
  • You should always review bills yourself before voting
  • These modules are experimental and under development
  • No AI system can perfectly represent your values

By using vote predictors, you acknowledge these limitations.
